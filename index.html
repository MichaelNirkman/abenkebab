The Best in Town

Aben Kebab is a high-quality restaurant for the quality-minded consumer.

The restaurant is located in an excellent spot between the Pasila train station and the Convention Centre.

Our first and foremost priority is to provide the best pizzas and kebab in the city, perhaps even the whole country.

We also serve dishes such as but not limited to: Salads, falafel, pasta and hamburgers. Everything we serve also comes with an alternative version to suit different diets, such as vegan and glucose-free versions.
